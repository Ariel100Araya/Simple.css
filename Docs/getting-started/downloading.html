Starting


To begin downloading Simple.css you need to go to its product page, and click download, it will bring you to one of two things, depending on your browsers ability to display files. Some browsers start the download automaticly while others will display the code. If your browser does display code instead of a download then you can press control+s (command+s for macOS) and save as Simple.css.
